About me






I'm Raul Andrade, a Computer Scientist from Brazil. I'm a PhD student in Decision Models and Health at UFPB and a researcher at the Laboratory of Technologies for Virtual Teaching and Statistics. My interests are Software Engineering, Virtual Reality, and Computers & Education (especially serious games and programming learning)

Projects Highlights

GANdido Portinari

Machine Learning class project at UFPB - 2020

Me and my colleagues used a CycleGAN to generate images that are similar to the paintings of Candido Portinari, a famous brazilian painter.

Bass tab generator

AI class project at UFPB - 2020

A project using LSTM networks to generate new bass tabs based on the Rage Against the Machine, Red Hot Chilli Peppers and Primus repertory.

Binary Bot

AI class project at UFPB - 2020

Consists in a Bot that trade options contracts based on previous data using a Time series approach in EUR/USD.

Medway Student Allocation

Project of Operations Research Class

Medway is a middle-sized (300 students) course at João Pessoa - PB. The problem here consists in allocating each student to one of the 15 advisors based on the time needed by the student and the estimated compatibility between each pair to let things balanced and not overload any advisor. The project was done using IBM CPLEX and some preprocessig libraries.

Helper Brasil Conta Comigo

The Brasil Conta Comigo is a program from the Brazilian Government to allocate students in med school final years into the public health sector to help with the COVID-19 Crisis. This project intuits to make it easier for these students to find their preferred places, filtering the data with the distance and the sector of work.

TAIL - Co-Founder and President

TAIL (Technology and Artificial Intelligence League) is a non-profit Academic League held at UFPB that aims to support the students knowledge sharing and to teach new students from all courses (not only the ones in the Computer Science, Engineering and Data Science department). The organization is advised by the Phds and professors at UFPB: Telmo Filho, Thais Gaudencio and Yuri Malheiros (responsibles for the ARIA laboratory).

CO-CEO REVISAI

Revisai is a plataform designed to provide an environment where students can do ENEM mock tests (Brazilian SAT). It was designed by UFPB undergraduate students and is already being commercialized. Revisai was developed to serve more than 700 students during the pandemics, to aid them in practicing for the ENEM and to revise the subjects

Medway - Advisor

Student Advisor for students pursuing a place in Brazilian public universities. In general, students that have concluded the high school or are in their last year. The job consists in scheduling the students studies and advising them in their activities.


LMF - Analyst

LMF (Financial Market League) is an academic league that studies the brazilian stock market. There I have done Companies fundamentalist analysis, software automation for the portifolio competion and Quantitative trading strategies with MQL5 and Python.

Logic Circuits I - Boolean Algebra - assistant instructor

A freshman class at UFPB that has the highest failling rates. Throughout the course I gave lectures and helped students with exercises and projects. Our work was granted a teaching initiation award by the University.
